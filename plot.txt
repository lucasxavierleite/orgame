The plot takes place in a magical land, a long time ago.

Our main character is a prisioner in the king's dungeon where a black magician
uses the prisioners as guinea pigs, trying to create a lethal weapon.

His experiments, however, fails and unleashes a great evil in the face of earth:
the moving shadow.

After a breakout, our prisioner must escape his cage and find shelter before the
shadow devour him. Although he doesn't know it yet, his role is very important
in the history of this land and, therefore, his burden is quite heavy...
